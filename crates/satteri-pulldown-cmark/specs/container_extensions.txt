# Directive tests (remark-directive compatible)
# Directives have no standard HTML/HAST representation, so they produce no HTML output.
# Only non-directive cases (invalid syntax) should produce visible output.

No name (bare :::) means not a directive:

```````````````````````````````` example_container_extensions
:::

content

:::
.
<p>:::</p>
<p>content</p>
<p>:::</p>
````````````````````````````````

No name (bare ::: with adjacent text):

```````````````````````````````` example_container_extensions
:::
Hi
:::
.
<p>:::
Hi
:::</p>
````````````````````````````````

Container directive produces no HTML:

```````````````````````````````` example_container_extensions
:::note
Content here
:::
.
````````````````````````````````

Container with label produces no HTML:

```````````````````````````````` example_container_extensions
:::note[Title]
Content
:::
.
````````````````````````````````

Container with attributes produces no HTML:

```````````````````````````````` example_container_extensions
:::warning{.big}
Be careful!
:::
.
````````````````````````````````

Nested containers produce no HTML:

```````````````````````````````` example_container_extensions
::::outer
:::inner
Content
:::
::::
.
````````````````````````````````

Leaf directive produces no HTML:

```````````````````````````````` example_container_extensions
::video[Title]{src=video.mp4}
.
````````````````````````````````

Text directive in paragraph:

```````````````````````````````` example_container_extensions
A :abbr[HTML]{title="HyperText Markup Language"} example.
.
<p>A  example.</p>
````````````````````````````````

Bare text directive (no label/attrs):

```````````````````````````````` example_container_extensions
This :smile is text.
.
<p>This  is text.</p>
````````````````````````````````

Emoji-style colon pair is not a directive:

```````````````````````````````` example_container_extensions
Hello :smile: world
.
<p>Hello :smile: world</p>
````````````````````````````````

Container with multiple paragraphs:

```````````````````````````````` example_container_extensions
:::note
Paragraph 1

Paragraph 2
:::
.
````````````````````````````````

Empty container:

```````````````````````````````` example_container_extensions
:::note
:::
.
````````````````````````````````

Container followed by content (directive itself produces no output):

```````````````````````````````` example_container_extensions
**bold after**
:::example
Content
:::
.
<p><strong>bold after</strong></p>
````````````````````````````````

Longer fence nesting:

```````````````````````````````` example_container_extensions
:::::foo
Hi
:::
::::::
.
````````````````````````````````

Container in blockquote:

```````````````````````````````` example_container_extensions
> :::foo
> Hi
.
<blockquote>
</blockquote>
````````````````````````````````

Name with underscores:

```````````````````````````````` example_container_extensions
:::c_d
Hi
:::
.
````````````````````````````````

Container can't be closed from inside blockquote:

```````````````````````````````` example_container_extensions
:::container
> shouldn't close, right?
> :::
> x
:::
.
````````````````````````````````

Nested containers with partial close:

```````````````````````````````` example_container_extensions
:::a
::::b
:::::c
::::
x
.
````````````````````````````````

Closing fence must be on its own line:

```````````````````````````````` example_container_extensions
:::a
content :::
.
````````````````````````````````

Multiple containers:

```````````````````````````````` example_container_extensions
:::a
:::b

:::
:::
.
````````````````````````````````

Longer opening/closing fence:

```````````````````````````````` example_container_extensions
::::a
:::b

:::
::::
.
````````````````````````````````

Shorter inner fence:

```````````````````````````````` example_container_extensions
:::a
::::b

::::
:::
.
````````````````````````````````

Space between colons and name is not a directive:

```````````````````````````````` example_container_extensions
::: note
Hi
:::
.
<p>::: note
Hi
:::</p>
````````````````````````````````

Leaf directive name only:

```````````````````````````````` example_container_extensions
::break
.
````````````````````````````````
